About this game

Valorant Mobile brings the action-packed, tactical gameplay of Riot Games' hit first-person shooter to mobile devices. In this game, every match tests your strategic thinking, reflexes, and teamwork.


Choose from a roster of unique agents, each equipped with special abilities that cater to different playstyles, whether you're an aggressive attacker, a stealthy defender, or a supportive teammate.
